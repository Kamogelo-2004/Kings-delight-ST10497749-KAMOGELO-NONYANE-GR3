## Project overview
In this proposal we are planning to create and design a developed website for King’s Delights Bakery, a small local online business. The goal is to develop a useful and aesthetically pleasing website that advertises the bakery’s goods and attracts more customers. King’s Delights is a small business that focuses on freshly baked goods like biscuits, pastries, cakes, cupcakes, and delicious desserts. It serves both locals and out-reach people. King’s Delights accepts special orders for all kinds of occasions like birthdays, weddings, and graduation orders. King’s Delights began as a small home-based bakery, driven by a passion for baking and a love for creating delicious, high-quality treats. The business started by supplying baked goods to family, friends, and local customers through word-of-mouth and social media platforms. With a focus on creativity and unique flavours, King’s Delights quickly gained popularity within the community. As demand for its products grew, the business expanded its operations by taking on more custom orders for special occasions such as birthdays, weddings, and celebrations. This growth allowed King’s Delights to build a strong customer base and establish a reputation for quality and reliability. 

 ## Mission and Vision
King’s Delights is committed to creating high-quality, freshly baked goods that bring joy to every customer. ## Part 2-CSS
The CSS used in the Kings Delights website improves both the appearance and usability of the site. It ensures a consistent layout by removing default browser styles and uses Flexbox and Grid to organise content neatly. The navigation is styled and centered for easy access, with hover and active effects to enhance user interaction. Images are made responsive, and forms are designed to be clear and user-friendly. Additional styling such as colours, typography, and buttons helps create a clean and professional look. Overall, the CSS makes the website visually appealing, responsive, and easy to use.I used media queries to apply to different screens sizes when opening or visiting my website
