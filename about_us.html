Welcome to Kings Delights

Our Story

Kings Delights specialises in freshly baked goods such as biscuits, pastries, cakes, cupcakes, and delicious fruity desserts. The business proudly serves local customers and offers custom orders for all occasions.

History

Kings Delights began as a home-based business after the owner, Kamogelo, decided to follow her passion for baking. Starting with family, friends, and local customers, the business quickly grew through word-of-mouth and social media.

As demand increased, the business expanded into a retail space, allowing it to reach a wider audience. Today, Kings Delights continues to grow, with plans to expand further both in-store and online.

Mission

To provide high-quality, freshly baked goods that bring joy and satisfaction to every customer, while continuously growing into a trusted and innovative bakery brand.

Vision

To become a well-known and respected bakery brand recognised for creativity, quality, and excellent customer service.
