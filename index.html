Welcome to Kings Delights

King’s Delights focuses on freshly baked goods such as cakes, cupcakes, and delicious fruity desserts. We proudly serve local customers and beyond, offering special orders for all occasions.

At King’s Delights, we believe every moment deserves something special. Whether you are celebrating a birthday, planning an event, or simply treating yourself, our goal is to bring joy and flavour to every occasion.

We cater for functions and events, including customised birthday and celebration cakes. Payments can also be made online.
